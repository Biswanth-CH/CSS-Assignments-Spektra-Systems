iPhone Buying Guide: Which Model is Right for You?

October 20, 2023

Choosing the right iPhone can be overwhelming with so many models available. This guide breaks down the key differences between the iPhone 15, iPhone 14, and older models to help you make the best decision.

Key Factors to Consider:

  • Budget: How much are you willing to spend?
  • Camera: Do you need advanced camera features like ProRAW or telephoto zoom?
  • Performance: Are you a power user who needs the latest A17 Pro chip?
  • Size: Do you prefer a compact phone or a larger display?
